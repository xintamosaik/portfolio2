early nerd

It all started with Basic. When I was a kid, my parents got me a Commodore 64. I fell into a trance back then and never got out. The games, tools, programs, learning Basic and screwing up direct memory access, all of it fascinated me and set me on a journey of curiosity and discovery in the world of computing.

Today, I stand as a web developer with a scientist's curiosity and an inventor's zeal, constantly intrigued by the underlying mechanics of the digital world. I take pleasure in crafting automation tools for even the simplest tasks, always chasing the joy of creating efficient, precise, and elegant solutions.
